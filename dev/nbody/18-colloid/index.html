COLLOIDAL PHASE TRANSITIONS

A toy model of diamegnetic and paramagnetic colloidal particles suspended in fluid, subject to an imposed magnetic field. As the direction of the magnetic field and the temperature varies, transitions occur between a fluid and several solid phases. Particles are coloured by temperature and by modified order parameter.

Based on Phase Transformations in Binary Colloidal Monolayers, Ye Yang et al. 2015.

  • ↑/↓: adjust temperature.
  • ←/→: adjust magnetic field direction.
